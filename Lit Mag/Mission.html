Dear Reader,

Thank you for enjoying the complex nature of multicultural humor with us. Feed The Kitty's goal is simple: create a vast hub for dirty, vulgar, and even offensive humor from around the world. Not only do we strongly believe that the most insightful cultural idiosyncracies are elucidated in a culture's joke structure, we also affirm that sexual humor creates the deepest cultural connections. Through sexual innuendo we are granted a vast window into our neighboring cultures, and often given a reflection on other's view of ourselves. Our mission is to provide an exceptionally meaningful translation for all of our jokes; a translation into a myriad of languages and with a hefty explanation of the slang and idioms within. One immense challenge is the idea of "meaning from what is missing": the idea that often times the joke is funnier and more meaningful with only subtle hints to the true subject matter. When dealing with foreign language, these ideas will be nearly impossible for the average learner to ascertain. This, amongst many other language barrier shortcommings, is the precise purpose of our existence. Join us on this journey spanning enormous distances. You will not only discover wonderful foreign quips, but also gain a deeper understanding of the human race.

Thank you,

Tim Brahm
